Working in Quantum Computing and Programming research group. Projects worked on: designed a new quantum programming language NDQJava based on Java. It is a hybrid language which introduces quantum components according to the basic principles of quantum mechanics. And also the quantum transmission components are introduced in order to improve quantum programming. Then we designed its processing system. The system takes advantage of the available processing system of Java, and mainly processes the quantum components. It consists of lexical analyzer, syntax analyzer,code generation, quantum assembler and quantum interpreter.
